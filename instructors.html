About Us

Welcome to Tiger Martial Arts, Sharjah’s premier training centre for martial arts and fitness. We offer classes in Karate, Jiu-jitsu, Judo, Muay Thai, and self-defence for all age groups and skill levels. Our facility includes a fully-equipped gym, steam room, sauna, and professional-grade mats.
